News

Here you will find an overview of the latest news in the context of PCOR-MII.

2024

14/05/2024: Workshop in Berlin

On May 14, a workshop on Patient Reported Outcome Measures (PROMs) took place at the TMF. Among other things, the role of PROMs in MII was discussed and the PCOR-MII project was presented. It was also discussed how PROMs can be recorded interoperably on a large scale using readily available tools, methods and models and used across locations and projects in the MII. Find out more on LinkedIn!

30/04/2024: Kick-Off Meeting

The virtual kick-off meeting for PCOR-MII took place on 30.04.2024. All team members came together to initiate and plan the first phase of the project.

01/03/2024: Official start

The PCOR-MII project officially started on 01.03.2024. We will report regularly on the progress of the project on this website.
